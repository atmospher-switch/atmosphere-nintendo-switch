Download Files

Download the latest Atmosphere package and extract the files to your computer. Ensure you have all required files before proceeding.

2

Prepare SD Card

Format your microSD card to FAT32 or exFAT. Copy the downloaded files to the root of your SD card following the provided structure.

3

Boot Custom Firmware

Insert the SD card into your Switch, enter RCM mode, and inject the payload using your preferred method to boot Atmosphere.

Once booted, verify that Atmosphere is running correctly by checking the system information and testing basic homebrew functionality.
