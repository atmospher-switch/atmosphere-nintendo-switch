What is Atmosphere Nintendo Switch?

Atmosphere Nintendo Switch is the most popular and widely used custom firmware (CFW) for the Nintendo Switch console. It enables users to run homebrew applications, install custom themes, backup game saves, and unlock advanced features that aren't available in the standard Nintendo operating system.

Developed and maintained by an active community, Atmosphere offers a stable and feature-rich platform for Switch enthusiasts who want to explore the full potential of their gaming console while maintaining compatibility with official firmware updates.
