About Atmosphere Switch

Empowering Nintendo Switch users with knowledge and tools

Who We Are

Atmosphere Switch is a comprehensive resource platform dedicated to providing accurate, up-to-date information about custom firmware and homebrew applications for the Nintendo Switch console. We are passionate about helping users understand and safely utilize the full capabilities of their gaming hardware.

Our team consists of experienced developers, tech enthusiasts, and dedicated community members who have been working with Nintendo Switch custom firmware since its early days. We believe in making technology accessible and understandable for everyone, regardless of their technical expertise.
